title: The Seamstress' Patchwork Reality
date: 2023-03-03
---

Tethered, ringing,  
broken bones,  
each had sound  
their hollow tone.

Lead's the servant,  
of these observant,  
eyes that fled,  
the ghastly seams.

And found themselves  
lost trapped in dreams,  
in the seamstress  
and her scenery.
