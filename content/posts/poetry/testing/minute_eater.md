You wore a sorry smile,
With face eclipsed,
By a phantom kiss,
That you barely missed.

You burned your time,
Then cracked your wrists,
And crossed my name,
Off from this list.

With questions found,
But no answers fixed,
The nightmares stung,
Like a needle pricks.
