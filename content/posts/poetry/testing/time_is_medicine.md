title: Time Is Medicine, But She Has Contraindications
date: 2023-03-03
---

She talked to me,
But I was just talking to the wine.

I was treading carpet,
But she was drawing sandy lines.

I was quiet,
And she was just looking for a fight.

She was fishing me,
When I saw the bait,
I decided that I'll bite.
